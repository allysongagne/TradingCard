Giant Panda

The eyespots on a giant panda are initially a circle but as the panda matures the circle changes to a teardrop

  • Scientific Name:Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 4 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Remote Mountain Regions

Giant Pandas in China are thought of as a national treasure. Their diet consist mainly of bamboo. On average a single Giant Panda can consume 20lbs of bamboo in a day.
